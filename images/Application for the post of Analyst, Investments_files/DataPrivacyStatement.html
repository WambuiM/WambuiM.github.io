Company Data Privacy Statement

FSD Africa is committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019 and other relevant standards. Any information you provide—such as contact details, CVs, and employment history—will be used strictly for recruitment purposes and accessed only by authorized persons.

We take appropriate security measures to safeguard your data, only retaining it as long as needed for recruitment. We will not share your information with third parties unless legally required or essential for the recruitment process.

By using our portal, you consent to the collection and processing of your data. For inquiries or to exercise your data rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer at dataprotection@fsdafrica.org
